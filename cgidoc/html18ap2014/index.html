About SubNucPred

SubnucPred is a combined method of two different aproachs which predict 10 different subnuclear locations: centromere, chromosome, nucleolus, nuclear envelope, nuclear speckles, telomere, nucleoplasm, nuclear matrix, pml, nuclear pore complex. First it predict nuclear proteins according to presence or absence of Pfam domain and if it could not find any nuclear domain then it predict according to amino acid composition of SVM module. The overall prediction accuracy of this subnucpred is 85.05% for centromere, 76.85% for chromosome, 81.79% for nucleolus, 81.27% for nuclear speckle, 79.37% for nuclear envelope, 83.33% for telomere, 88.89% for nuclear pore complex, 76.98% for nucleoplasm, 77.78% for nuclear matrix, 75.40% for PML-Body respectively.
